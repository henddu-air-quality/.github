## About Henddu

**Henddu** is a deep-tech environmental intelligence company developing integrated systems for monitoring, modelling, and forecasting **air quality and climate risk across Africa**.

By combining **satellite data**, **IoT sensor networks**, and **AI analytics**, we provide governments, industries, and research institutions with real-time insights into pollutant dynamics and related health risks — turning environmental data into actionable intelligence.

> *Today, fewer than 8 out of 54 African countries have an effective air quality monitoring network. Henddu is building the infrastructure to close that gap.*

---

## What We Do

| Capability | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Real-Time Monitoring** | 24/7 continuous tracking of air quality through connected sensors and satellite observations |
| **Integrated Data Intelligence** | Advanced SCAN analysis coupling pollution, traffic, and health data |
| **Predictive Forecasting** | AI-powered early warning systems for air quality trends |
| **Spatial Analysis** | Historical data analysis to detect pollution clusters and spatial trends |
| **Reporting & Compliance** | Customizable reports for businesses and governments to track emissions and meet regulatory requirements |
| **Public Awareness** | Support for governments in developing awareness campaigns and educational programs |
